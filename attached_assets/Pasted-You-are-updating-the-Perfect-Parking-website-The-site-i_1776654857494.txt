Add these questions and answers:
Q: How do I pay for parking?
A: Most Perfect Parking locations use scan-to-pay. Just scan the QR code
posted in the lot and follow the prompts — no app required.
Q: Do I need to download an app?
A: No. Just scan the QR code or text the number on the sign. It takes you
directly to a mobile payment page.
Q: What if I can't scan the QR code?
A: No problem — just text the number listed on the signs in the lot. We'll
send you a link to start your session.
Q: What do I need to display in my car?
A: Nothing. Your license plate is your permit. Just make sure you entered it
correctly when starting your session.
Q: How do I get a receipt or proof of payment?
A: A text confirmation is sent to the phone number you entered at checkout.
Check your text messages. If you didn't receive it, make sure you entered
the correct phone number — a typo at checkout is the most common reason it
doesn't arrive.
Q: I was charged incorrectly. How do I request a refund?
A: Email us at Support@PerfectParking.com with your full name, phone number,
email address, and the license plate associated with the parking session.
Our team will review and resolve it quickly.
Q: How can I purchase ongoing parking at the Laguna Madre Boat Ramp?
A: Scan the QR code on the sign at the lot. You'll see options for weekly,
monthly, and annual permits directly on the payment page.
Q: I haven't received my verification code. What should I do?
A: Check that the phone number shown on your screen is correct. If it is,
you can resend the code after 60 seconds. If it's wrong, tap "Change Phone
Number" and try again.
