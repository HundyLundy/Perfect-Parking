What to build

Add a prominent refund/billing support callout box to the Parkers page. Place it above the video and above the FAQ section, directly below the hero banner.

•

•

•

•

Callout box design spec

Visual style:

•
Background: #E8EFF7 (PP light blue)

•
Left border: 4px solid #1965B1 (PP primary blue)

•
Top label / badge: small gold pill badge (#DEC600 background, #00305b dark text) with text: "Refund or Billing Issue?"

•
Body text in #00305b (dark navy) using Open Sans

•
Email address support@perfectparking.com displayed in bold #1965B1 and styled as a mailto: link — when tapped on mobile it opens the native email app with the address pre-filled

•
The 6-item required fields list displayed as a clean bulleted list, not a wall of text

•
Fully responsive — must look clean on a phone screen

Content — render exactly this inside the box:




Refund or Billing Issue?

Email our support team: support@perfectparking.com

To get a faster response, include all of the following in your email:

• Your name
• Your phone number
• Your license plate number
• The parking location (lot name or address)
• The start or end date and time of your parking session
• Your parking session ID (sent to you in your confirmation text)

Missing any of these will delay our response — we'll need to follow up to collect the missing info before we can help.

•
Insert this callout box AFTER the hero section (the blue "Need Help With Your Parking Session?" banner) and BEFORE the video embed

•
It should be the first content element a user sees when they scroll past the headline
